World-Class Facility at Prabhu Orthopaedic Centre: Expert Care for Every Bone & Joint

When it comes to musculoskeletal health, receiving treatment in the right environment makes a world of difference. Prabhu Orthopaedic Centre, under the leadership of Dr. Chetan Patil S, offers a fully equipped, state-of-the-art facility that ensures comfort, accuracy, and excellent outcomes for every patient. Whether you’re seeking treatment for fractures, arthritis, sports injuries, or complex joint issues, our centre delivers personalized care with precision.

Recognized as the Best Orthopaedic Centre in Rajajinagar, Prabhu Orthopaedic Centre combines medical expertise, advanced technology, and compassionate service to provide one of the most trusted healthcare experiences in Bangalore.

A Trusted Name in Orthopaedic Excellence

Prabhu Orthopaedic Centre is not just a clinic—it’s a comprehensive orthopedic care facility designed around patient safety, precision diagnosis, and rapid recovery. With years of experience in treating a wide range of orthopedic conditions, Dr. Chetan Patil S, one of the Best Orthopaedic Doctors in Bangalore, leads a dedicated team committed to delivering world-class bone and joint care.

Our Facility: Where Technology Meets Compassion
✅ Expert Orthopaedic Consultation Rooms

Our consultation rooms are spacious, private, and designed to provide a calming atmosphere where patients can comfortably discuss their health concerns. Each consultation is conducted by Dr. Chetan Patil S, who takes the time to understand your condition in-depth, reviews previous medical history, and creates a personalized treatment roadmap tailored to your lifestyle and health goals.

✅ Digital Imaging and Diagnostics

At Prabhu Orthopaedic Centre, we believe that a precise diagnosis leads to effective treatment. Our facility is equipped with advanced diagnostic tools, including:

  • Digital X-Ray Machines – Quick, high-resolution imaging for immediate evaluation
  • Ultrasound and MRI Support – In partnership with diagnostic labs
  • Computer-Aided Joint Analysis – For accurate pre-operative planning

These technologies enable Dr. Chetan Patil S to visualize joint and bone issues clearly and recommend treatments with confidence and precision.

✅ Minor Procedure and Day Care Surgery Room

For treatments that don’t require hospitalization, we provide a safe, sterile, and fully equipped procedure room for:

  • Joint injections
  • Fracture reductions and plaster application
  • Tendon sheath injections
  • Removal of foreign bodies
  • Suture application/removal
  • Wound care and dressing

Each minor procedure is handled with utmost safety, patient comfort, and hygiene.

✅ Physiotherapy and Rehabilitation Unit

Recovery doesn’t end with surgery or medication. That’s why we’ve integrated a physiotherapy and rehabilitation unit within our facility. Here, trained physiotherapists work under the guidance of Dr. Chetan Patil S to support:

  • Post-operative rehabilitation
  • Strength training and flexibility improvement
  • Balance training for elderly patients
  • Sport-specific conditioning programs

Our rehab plans help patients regain mobility faster and reduce the chances of future injury.

✅ Dedicated Waiting Area and Reception

Your experience matters to us from the moment you step in. Our clean, air-conditioned waiting area offers a relaxed environment for patients and family members. The reception desk is managed by friendly, trained staff who assist with appointments, insurance support, follow-up scheduling, and other queries.

✅ Patient-Centric Design

From wheelchair accessibility to clear signage and patient education boards, every corner of Prabhu Orthopaedic Centre is designed with your needs in mind. Whether you’re a senior citizen with arthritis or a young athlete recovering from an ACL injury, our facility ensures a smooth and seamless experience.
