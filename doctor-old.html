About Doctor

Best in Bone & Joint Care – Trusted for Over 9 Years

Dr. Chetan Patil S

MBBS, MS (Orthopedics), Fellowship in Arthroscopy & Joint Replacement, Fellowship in Joint Replacement (Germany)

Dr. Chetan is an experienced orthopaedic surgeon with over nine years of expertise in managing both operative and non-operative treatments for bone and joint disorders, including fractures. His comprehensive approach ensures that patients receive evidence-based, personalized care tailored to their specific conditions and lifestyle demands.

He has received specialized training in joint replacement from Germany and advanced arthroscopic techniques from Mumbai, equipping him with cutting-edge skills to deliver world-class orthopedic care. His training enables him to offer modern, minimally invasive treatments that promote faster recovery, less pain, and better outcomes for his patients.

With extensive experience in treating sports injuries, Dr. Chetan has worked closely with athletes and active individuals to support their return to peak performance. Notably, he served as the First Team Doctor for Bengaluru Football Club during the 2023–24 season, providing on-field medical expertise and managing injury prevention and recovery protocols for professional players. This unique experience further reinforces his deep understanding of sports medicine and athletic rehabilitation at the highest level of competition.
